AquaticMonkey is an independent idea and initiative started in July of 2018 by Rahb Young, an International Studuies and Psychology student at Cazenovia College. This congregation of websites is meant to share and promote other's qualities and aspirations through AquaticMonkey web-development as well as provide an outlet for the creator to practice and develope their own skills in web-development. AquaticMonkey is made possible by Github and it's convenient public repositories. AquaticMonkey gives special thanks to KhanAcademy for providing the education and fundamentals to make this possible.

Visit Khan Academy to discover a wealth of information and walkthroughs on major curriculums and subjects
